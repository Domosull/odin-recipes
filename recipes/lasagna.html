Lasagna

Description

A delicious pasta dish from Italy. One of the oldest pasta dishes, it is easy to make and doesnt take long to cook.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets
  3. Make the ricotta
  4. Layer the meat and lasagna on top of each other
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Allow to rest before serving
