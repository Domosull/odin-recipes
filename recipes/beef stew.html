Beef Stew

Description

This Instant Pot stew recipe is the ultimate, hearty, melt-in-your-mouth comfort food. It's so easy to make in an Instant Pot for a simple midweek dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 pound beef chuck, cut into cubes
  • 4 potatoes, cut into cubes
  • mushrooms, cut in half
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 cloves of garlice, diced
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon worchestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • half teaspoon black pepper
  • half teaspoon rosemary

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Melt butter in pot, then add the beef and cook until browned
  3. Add potatoes, mushrooms, onion, cattos and garlic
  4. Cover with beef broth then add worchestershire sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper and rosemary
  5. Close and allow to cook for 40 minutes
